An app to get information about artificial additives found in Processed foods. You can search for additives by names or by E Codes (aka INS codes)
This app also has a barcode scanner to scan food barcodes and get their nutritional details from OpenFoodFacts database.
You can also search for a specific food product to get their details without having that product nearby.
This app supports a growing list of languages and offer automatic cloud translation for additive details.
